# Customer Interview Script — Phase 0

Purpose: Validate the problem hypothesis and discover pain points, prioritization, and willingness to pay.

Intro (30s)
- Thank you, introduce yourself and purpose.
- "We are exploring ways to help [persona] reduce X — this will take ~20 mins. Everything you say is anonymous and for research only."

Warm-up (2 min)
- Tell me about your role and a typical day.
- What tools do you use for [relevant area]?

Problem discovery (8 min)
- Walk me through the last time you experienced [problem]. What happened?
- How often does this happen? How much time does it cost?
- What workarounds do you currently use?

Impact & Priority (4 min)
- If this problem were solved, what would it enable you to do?
- How would you prioritize solving it vs other initiatives?

Solution & Willingness to Pay (4 min)
- If a product could do X, what would you expect it to do out of the box?
- How much would your organization pay for a solution like this? (range)

Wrap-up (2 min)
- Anything I didn't ask that you think matters?
- Can I follow up if we have quick clarifying questions?
